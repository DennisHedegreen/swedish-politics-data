# Sweden Public Vs Internal Boundary

Public now:

- municipality-safe party share for the 2022 Riksdag election
- municipality-safe turnout
- municipality-safe factor files for the first Swedish factor set
- public provenance manifest

Internal or not-ready:

- district-level raw workbook staging
- any finer-grained Swedish geography
- any factor family that still needs semantic checking
- any public comparability claim beyond the declared Sweden scope

Rule:

Sweden v1 is public as a Sweden-only reading layer, not as a broader comparison layer.
