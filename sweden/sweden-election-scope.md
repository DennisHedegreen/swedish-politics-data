# Sweden Election Scope

Current public scope:

- election type: `Riksdag`
- municipality election years: `2014`, `2018`, `2022`
- national trend years: `2002`, `2006`, `2010`, `2014`, `2018`, `2022`
- public geography: `municipality`
- source: `Valmyndigheten`

Why this scope:

- national parliamentary election is the cleanest first Swedish layer
- municipality-level aggregation is public-readable
- the public layer is easier to explain when one election family stays explicit

Not in scope yet:

- municipal elections
- regional elections
- broader non-Riksdag Sweden election families
