# Sweden Entity Schema

- `country_id`: `sweden`
- public display name: `Sweden`
- language in UI: `English`
- national election label: `Riksdag election`
- public geography type: `municipality`
- public geography source: official Swedish municipality codes

Important semantic rules:

- Sweden is its own implementation, not “Nordics generic”.
- Municipality names and codes remain Swedish-source truth, even when the UI is English.
- Party labels may be shown in Swedish, English or both, but the underlying party identity stays Sweden-specific.
